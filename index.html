AFHC – RKSK Health Management App (Govt. of India)


A large-scale government healthcare mobile application developed under the Rashtriya Kishore Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK), an initiative by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare (MoHFW), Government of India, with strategic support from the World Health Organization (WHO).

The application is designed for Adolescent Friendly Health Clinics (AFHCs) and focuses on adolescents aged 10–19 years. It enables digital registration, secure health data capture, monitoring, and reporting for multiple adolescent health domains including:

  • Nutrition & Anaemia Tracking
  • Reproductive & Sexual Health
  • Mental Health & Emotional Well-being
  • Substance Abuse Prevention
  • Menstrual Hygiene Management

The system acts as a decision support and monitoring platform, helping healthcare professionals efficiently record clinic visits, manage patient data, and improve service delivery. The project was implemented in laboratory districts as part of national-level digital health transformation.
